Oreo Truffles

Description

Oreo Truffles are a fan favorite and super easy to make, which is why I always bring them to gatherings or holiday parties. With only 3 ingredients, these are great to make last minute or with kids!

Ingredients
  • 1 package of Oreos
  • 1 80z brick of cream cheese
  • Your favorite flavor of chocolate chips for melting
Steps
  1. Finely crush oreos. This can either be done by hand or with a food processor
  2. Add crushed oreos to a medium bowl, add cream cheese to the bowl, combine
  3. After ingredients are well combined, roll into 1in balls and place on a wax paper lined cookie sheet
  4. Put oreo balls in the refrigerator while we melt the chocolate
  5. Prepare a double boiler to melt your chocolate chips
  6. When chocolate is melted, toss in oreo balls one at a time until they are well covered in chocolate. Put the truffle on the wax paper to cool.
  7. When all truffles are coated, place them back in the fridge where they should be stored.
  8. Enjoy!
